Citation Style Used

Chicago Manual of Style

Notes on Personal Names

In the Historical Notes section of each entry, the order of personal names follows the convention of each individual's native language/country. For example, the names of Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese individuals are written in the order: surname/family name first, followed by given name(s).

Other Notes

The LSA uses traditional, rather than simplified, Chinese characters.

Romanization Styles Used

  • Arabic: International Journal of Middle East Studies
  • Bengali: ALA-Library of Congress
  • Chinese: Hanyu Pinyin
  • Japanese: Modified Hepburn
  • Korean: Revised Romanization of Korean (developed by the National Academy of the Korean Language)
  • Persian: ALA-Library of Congress
  • Urdu: ALA-Library of Congress
